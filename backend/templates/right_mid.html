What is Instaright?

The fast, easy and effort-free way to save, discover and share information all over web. Easy to start, easy to use. Organizes and streamlines infromation into categories providing clear presentation.

Save: Instaright browser plugins

Instaright Firefox addon and Chrome Extension

Discover: Realtime Notifications

Discover infromation in realtime article feed or via Instant messenger. Subscribe to categories and topics of your interest. Instaright provides updates on your favorite IM client.

Share: Instaright on social media

Instaright detects highly engaged content and leverage it across social networks
